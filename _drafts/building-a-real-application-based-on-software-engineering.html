Main goals

The major goal here is to understand how software engineering works not just on paper but doing it creating scripts, understand concepts, implementing new techniques and so on.

Also I have a personal goal to improve the practice of software engineering, I saw everyday programmers complaining about wrong process while developing an application, I think this could be a good start in how to do it right.

What we will get of all this ? Sharing knowledge of course and learning as well. Developers are always smart in solve problems in algorithms, math and so on but they lack in communication and business need, often those words come together.
